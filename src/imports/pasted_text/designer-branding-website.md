Create a modern, minimal, and premium personal branding website UI for a professional graphic designer.

The website should focus on:
- Personal branding
- Portfolio showcase
- Selling design services

---

Style Direction:
- Minimal, clean, lots of white space
- Premium and modern look
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Grid-based layout
- Apple-level simplicity and elegance

---

Brand Colors:
- Primary Accent: #E1FE5D (use for subtle highlights and accents)
- Secondary Accent: #9F74FF (use for buttons, CTAs, and key actions)

Color Rules:
- Use white or very light background
- Use black or dark gray for text
- Do not overuse accent colors
- Keep everything clean and balanced
- Optional: use a subtle gradient (#9F74FF to #E1FE5D) for highlights

---

Target Audience:
- Bangladesh-based clients (primary)
- International clients (secondary)

---

Pages to Design:
1. Home
2. About Me
3. Services
4. Portfolio
5. Blog
6. Pricing

Also include:
- Terms & Conditions page
- Privacy Policy page

---

Header:
- Left: Logo
- Right: Navigation (Home, About Me, Services, Portfolio, Blog, Pricing)
- CTA Button: “Start a Project” (visually highlighted)

---

Popup Modal (Start a Project):
Design a clean, modern popup form with smooth appearance.

Fields:
- Full Name
- Email
- WhatsApp Number (required)
- Select Service (dropdown)
- Select Budget (USD range)
- Preferred Contact Method
- Project Description

---

Home Page Sections:

1. Hero Section:
- Left: Headline, subtext, CTA buttons
- Right: Designer image placeholder
- Headline: “Minimal Design. Maximum Impact.”
- Subtext: Personal branding and mockup design expert
- Buttons: Start a Project, View Portfolio

2. Services Preview:
- 3–4 minimal cards:
  - Personal Branding
  - Brand Identity
  - Social Media Design
  - Mockup Design

3. Portfolio Highlight:
- Clean grid layout
- Subtle hover effect

4. Why Choose Me:
- 3–4 simple points with minimal icons

5. Process Section:
- Step-by-step layout:
  - Submit Project
  - Discussion
  - Design
  - Delivery

6. Pricing Preview:
- 2–3 clean pricing cards

7. Final CTA Section:
- Strong call to action encouraging users to start a project

---

About Page:
- Introduction
- Personal journey/story
- Skills and expertise
- Why choose me
- CTA section

---

Services Page:
- Each service in separate section
- Include description, features, and target audience
- CTA after each service

---

Portfolio Page:
- Minimal grid layout
- Category filter (Branding, Mockup, Social)
- Clean spacing and alignment

---

Blog Page:
- Blog cards with image, title, and short description
- Clean and readable layout

---

Pricing Page:
- 3 packages (Basic, Standard, Premium)
- Include price, features, delivery time
- CTA button for each

---

Footer:
- Quick links
- Services
- Legal (Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policy)
- Social media links

---

Animation & Interaction Guidelines:
- Use subtle fade-in and slide-up animations
- Smooth hover effects on buttons, cards, and portfolio items
- Slight scale and shadow on hover
- Clean and modern transitions
- Avoid heavy or flashy animations
- Focus on smooth and premium user experience

---

Overall Goal:
Design a clean, high-converting, and premium personal brand website that builds trust and attracts clients while maintaining a minimal aesthetic.
